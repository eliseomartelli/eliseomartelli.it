## Bypass TPM and RAM:

`SHIFT+F10`, `regedit`, `HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\Setup`.
Right click and add a new key named `LabConfig`.

Right click in `LabConfig` and add two 32-bit DWORDs:

- `BypassTPMCheck`
- `BypassRAMCheck`

Set them both to 1.

## Skip Microsoft Account

`SHIFT+F10`, `ipconfig \release` or `ncpa.cpl` and disable all network
connections, `oobe\bypassnro`. 
Just click "I don't have internet" during setup.
