Usefulness, understandability, simplicity.  
If you ever approached the work of [Dieter Rams](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dieter_Rams), these principles should be familiar to you.

These are the principles we should focus ourselves on when designing and building modern systems.
Reducing complexity in modern systems is not an easy task.
